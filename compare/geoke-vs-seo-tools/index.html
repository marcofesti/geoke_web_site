Geoke vs Traditional SEO Tools

SEMrush, Ahrefs, and Moz were built for a world where Google was the only answer engine. That world is over.

Traditional SEO tools are exceptional at what they were built for: keyword rankings, backlinks, and Google SERP tracking. But AI search doesn't work like Google.

AI systems don't rank pages. They synthesize answers. The signals that make you authoritative to Google are different from the signals that make AI trust and cite your brand.
