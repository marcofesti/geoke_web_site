Enterprise Agreement

Last updated: April 7, 2026

This Enterprise Agreement supplements the Terms of Service and governs use of the Geoke Enterprise plan.

1. Scope

This Agreement applies to customers subscribing to the Geoke Enterprise plan and any custom enterprise arrangements agreed in writing with Geoke.

2. Authorized Users

Enterprise customers may add users to their workspace up to the limit specified in their subscription. Each user must agree to Geoke's Terms of Service. Client accounts managed within the Client Center are subject to the agency customer's agreement with their own clients. The agency customer is responsible for ensuring proper authorization from end clients.

3. Data Processing

For Enterprise customers processing personal data of EU or UK residents, Geoke will enter into a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) upon request. Contact legal@geoke.ai.

4. SLA

Enterprise customers receive a 99.5% uptime SLA for the core platform. In the event of downtime exceeding this threshold, customers may request service credits.

5. Custom Pricing

Enterprise pricing for teams exceeding standard plan limits is available upon request. Contact sales@geoke.ai for custom Enterprise pricing.

6. Contact

Enterprise inquiries: enterprise@geoke.ai
