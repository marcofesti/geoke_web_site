Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Permitted Uses

  • Using the Service to analyze AI visibility for domains you own or are authorized to analyze.
  • Using the Service to generate content for domains you own or manage.
  • Using the Service in an agency capacity to analyze and optimize client domains, provided clients have authorized you to do so.
  • Exporting reports and data for internal or client-facing purposes.

Prohibited Uses

  • Analyzing domains without authorization from the domain owner.
  • Using the Service to generate spam, misleading content, or content designed to manipulate AI systems through deceptive means.
  • Reverse engineering, scraping, or attempting to extract the underlying AI models or query infrastructure.
  • Using the Service in a manner that violates applicable laws, regulations, or third-party rights.
  • Attempting to circumvent rate limits, access controls, or platform security measures.
  • Sharing account credentials with individuals outside your authorized team.

Consequences of Violation

Violation of this policy may result in suspension or termination of your account. We reserve the right to terminate accounts in violation of this policy without prior notice.
