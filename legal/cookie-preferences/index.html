Cookie Policy & Preferences

Last updated: April 7, 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They allow the website to recognize your device, remember your preferences, and understand how you use the site.

Cookies we use

Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for the platform to function. Cannot be disabled. Examples: authentication tokens, session management, security cookies.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the platform (pages visited, features used). We use privacy-respecting analytics. You may opt out.

Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences (language, display settings). You may opt out, though some features may not function correctly.

Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertising and measure campaign performance on geoke.ai. You may opt out.

Managing your preferences

You can update your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the 'Cookie Preferences' link in the footer. Most browsers also allow you to manage cookies through browser settings. Note: Disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent you from logging in to the Geoke platform.

Third-party cookies

We use a limited number of third-party services that may set cookies: analytics providers, payment processors, and support tooling. These services are contractually required to use your data only to provide their services to us.
