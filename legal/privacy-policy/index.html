Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, company name, and billing information.

Domain and Competitive Data: When you use the Service, you provide domain names, competitor domains, and business category information. This data is used solely to generate your AI visibility analysis.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the Service, including pages viewed, features used, and actions taken.

Communications: If you contact us, we retain those communications to assist you and improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

To provide, operate, and improve the Service. To generate AI visibility analysis, reports, and content recommendations. To send you product updates, security notices, and support communications. To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your domain or business data to train AI models.

3. AI Query Anonymization

When the Geoke platform submits queries to external AI systems (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, Grok, Microsoft Copilot) on your behalf, these queries are anonymized before transmission. Proprietary business information and personally identifiable information are not included in prompts sent to external AI systems.

4. Data Sharing

We share data with third-party service providers only as necessary to operate the Service (e.g., cloud infrastructure providers, payment processors). All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data. We may disclose data if required by law or in connection with a business transaction such as a merger or acquisition.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting privacy@geoke.ai.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit, AES-256 encryption at rest, and role-based access controls. We conduct regular security reviews.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@geoke.ai. EU and UK residents have additional rights under GDPR. California residents have rights under CCPA.

8. Contact

For privacy inquiries: privacy@geoke.ai — Geoke, Inc., San Francisco, CA, United States.
