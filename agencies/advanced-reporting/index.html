Enterprise reporting tier

What's different at the Enterprise level.

Advanced Reporting is available on Enterprise and goes far beyond basic dashboards.

01

Branded Client Reports

Export polished PDFs with your agency logo and brand colors. Every report looks like it came from you — because it does.

02

Multi-client Dashboard

All clients visible from one workspace. Monitor AI Authority Scores across your entire portfolio without switching accounts.

03

Comparative Reporting

Month-over-month AI Authority Score tracking with trend analysis. Show clients exactly how their visibility is improving over time.

04

Implementation Audit Trail

Every page, FAQ addition, and schema markup change logged with timestamps. Full accountability for every action taken on a client's behalf.

05

AI Question Opportunity Reports

Ranked lists of AI questions your clients should be answering, with demand and opportunity scores. Know exactly where to focus content efforts first.

06 — Enterprise only

Guided AI Audit (GAT)

A structured methodology that walks your team through every step of a full AI visibility audit — ensuring consistent, high-quality deliverables every time.
