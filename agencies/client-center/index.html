Client Center

Manage every client's AI visibility from one place.

The Client Center is your agency's command center for AI search.

Book a Demo See Enterprise pricing →
One workspace. Every client.

Stop juggling separate logins.

Stop juggling separate logins and disconnected reports for each client. The Client Center gives your agency a single, structured command center for managing every client's AI search presence.

01

Portfolio Overview

A single-screen view of all clients' AI Authority Scores, visibility trends, and alerts. Understand the health of every engagement at a glance.

02

Per-client workspaces

Every client gets a dedicated, fully isolated workspace. Data, settings, and reports are completely separated — no cross-contamination, no confusion.

03

Team permissions

Role-based access control for Analyst, Manager, and Admin roles. Assign team members to specific clients with precisely the right level of access.

04

Automated alerts

Set custom AI Authority Score thresholds for each client. Get notified immediately when a score drops — so you can act before your client notices.

05

Client-facing reporting

Grant clients a secure, read-only view of their own dashboard — or export a branded PDF. Transparency that builds trust without giving away the kitchen.

06

Bulk actions

Run visibility analysis, refresh scores, or generate reports across multiple clients simultaneously. Built for efficiency at scale.

Availability

Built for agencies managing 3+ active client accounts.

The Client Center is available exclusively on the Enterprise plan. It's engineered for agencies running serious GEO practices — not solo users managing a single domain.
