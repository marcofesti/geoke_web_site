Two connectors. One workflow.

Geoke's native connectors bridge the gap between AI visibility intelligence and live website implementation — no intermediary steps required.

W
GKE Connector for WordPress
Available Now

The official Geoke plugin for WordPress. Connects your Geoke account directly to your WordPress installation and deploys AI optimizations to the right pages automatically.

What It Does
Automatically injects AI-optimized FAQ schema markup into target pages
Deploys AI Content Blueprint sections as structured page content
Updates meta descriptions and structured data to improve AI citation potential
Applies per-page recommendations from the Site AI Optimization Engine in one click
Syncs back to Geoke for automatic score recalculation after each deployment
Setup
  1. 01 Install the Geoke plugin from the WordPress plugin directory or upload directly from your dashboard.
  2. 02 Connect your Geoke account by pasting your API key into the plugin settings — takes under 60 seconds.
  3. 03 Geoke scans your WordPress site, maps pages to opportunities, and presents a deployment queue ready to approve.
  4. 04 Approve and deploy. Changes go live immediately and Geoke logs each implementation with before/after scores.
S
GKE Connector for Shopify
Available Now

The official Geoke app for Shopify. Connects your store directly to your Geoke account and deploys AI optimizations across product pages, collections, and store content automatically.

What It Does
Adds AI-optimized product Q&A and FAQ schema to product and collection pages
Deploys AI Content Blueprint copy to product descriptions and store pages
Optimizes meta titles and descriptions for AI citation relevance
Injects structured data that helps AI systems understand your product catalog accurately
Syncs implementation results back to Geoke for automatic score recalculation
Setup
  1. 01 Install the Geoke app from the Shopify App Store or add directly from your Geoke dashboard.
  2. 02 Authorize the connection in Shopify admin — read and write permissions for content and metafields only.
  3. 03 Geoke audits your store, maps product and collection pages to opportunities, and builds your deployment queue.
  4. 04 Review and approve changes. Geoke deploys to your store and logs each update with impact scores.
