The Problem

Standard AI visibility tools ask generic questions. Geoke simulates the real people who are actually asking.

Most tools send a list of broad keywords to AI platforms and call it visibility tracking. The problem: your customers don't search like that. They search with context, intent, industry-specific framing, and real-world urgency — and AI responds very differently to those queries.

IDPBS builds structured digital twins of your target customer segments and generates the real queries they're actually asking. The result is visibility data that reflects your actual market position, not a generic average.

How It Works

Four steps from persona to insight.

01

Build Digital Twins

Geoke constructs structured user profiles for each of your target customer segments. Each Digital Twin includes industry, company size, role, buying stage, region, and key pain points — giving AI queries the context they need to surface real answers.

02

Generate Intent-Specific Queries

For each Digital Twin, Geoke generates dozens of realistic, context-specific queries reflecting actual search behavior. These are not keyword permutations — they're the real questions your customers are asking AI right now.

03

Submit to All AI Systems

Every query is submitted to all six major AI platforms: ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, Grok, and Copilot. Geoke captures whether your brand appears, where it ranks, and how it's described in each response.

04

Identify Niche Visibility Gaps

The results reveal which customer personas find your brand in AI — and which ones don't. You see exactly which queries, platforms, and segments represent your biggest gaps and biggest opportunities.

FAQ

Common questions about IDPBS.

How are Digital Twins built — do I need to provide data?

Geoke builds Digital Twins from your onboarding inputs — your industry, target segments, key use cases, and competitors. You can define as many personas as your plan allows, and refine them at any time. No proprietary customer data is required, though you can enrich twins with your own segment descriptions if you choose.

How many queries does IDPBS simulate per run?

The number of queries depends on your plan and the number of Digital Twins you've configured. Typically, Geoke generates 8–15 unique queries per persona per simulation cycle. Queries are refreshed regularly to reflect evolving AI behavior, and you can trigger manual re-runs at any time from the dashboard.

Which AI platforms are included in the simulation?

IDPBS submits queries to all six major AI platforms: ChatGPT (OpenAI), Gemini (Google), Claude (Anthropic), Perplexity, Grok (xAI), and Microsoft Copilot. Each platform returns independent results, and Geoke tracks your brand's visibility across all of them simultaneously for each query and persona.

How is IDPBS different from standard keyword-based AI tracking?

Standard AI tracking tools submit a fixed list of broad keywords and report back whether your brand appeared. IDPBS generates the kind of layered, context-specific queries that reflect real customer intent — including role, industry, geography, buying stage, and urgency. This surfaces visibility gaps that broad-keyword tracking completely misses, and provides actionable intelligence at the segment level rather than the domain level.
