Trust

Your data is your data.
We take that seriously.

How Geoke handles your business information, AI queries, and client data.

Your domain data

Stays yours

We do not sell, share, or use your domain analysis data for training purposes. It's yours.

AI queries

Anonymized

When Geoke submits queries to AI systems on your behalf, we anonymize them before submission. No PII included.

Client data

Fully isolated

In the Enterprise tier, each client account is fully isolated. Your client's data is never visible to other accounts.

Connectors

Content only

WordPress and Shopify connectors are scoped to content only. No access to orders, customers, or financial data.

Security

Infrastructure we're proud of.

  • SOC 2 Type II compliance in progress (target: Q4 2026)
  • All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Role-based access controls across all account tiers
  • Regular third-party security audits
  • Infrastructure hosted on ISO 27001-certified cloud providers

For security inquiries: security@geoke.ai
