About

We built what
we couldn't find.

Geoke is an AI search authority platform founded in San Francisco by a team that saw the shift happening before most people were paying attention.

The origin

In 2023, our team was managing digital marketing for mid-market companies across Europe and the US. We started noticing something: clients were asking why their carefully optimized websites weren't showing up when their customers asked ChatGPT about their industry.

We looked for tools to answer that question. We found monitoring dashboards. We found spreadsheets. We found people guessing.

We decided to build the platform we needed: one that didn't just measure AI visibility, but explained it, strategized around it, and fixed it.

That's Geoke.

What we believe

AI search is the most consequential shift in brand visibility since the invention of Google.

Analytics without action is just expensive anxiety.

The best marketing tools do the work, not just report on it.

Every brand deserves to be heard in AI-generated answers — from a two-person startup to a Fortune 500.

The team

Geoke is led by Marco Festi (CEO), based in San Francisco. Our team spans product, AI research, and growth — with deep roots in both digital marketing and machine learning.

We're a small team building a significant platform. We move fast, we care about quality, and we're obsessed with the problem we're solving.
